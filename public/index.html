Short Touch

Make it. Touch it.

Every MacBook Pro since 2016 now has the infamous Touch Bar. Hate it or love it, it is there. The greatest drawback is not its lack of capability but more so its lack of integration. Not many applications use it to its fullest extent and so it just sits there. Short Touch attempts to give a user a quick way to launch their favorite web pages and applications at the touch of a button.
